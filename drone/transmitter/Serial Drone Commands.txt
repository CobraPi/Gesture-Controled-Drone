Drone Serial Commands

1 - Bind to drone
2 - Unbind from drone
3 - Reconnect to drone
5 - Switch from joystick to accelerometer control (default = joystick)
8 - Display joystick values
9 - Display accelerometer data (pitch, roll)
0 - Display PWM payload 

c - calibrate accelerometer 


* Make sure to hold joystick throttle down when starting (towards headers)0000
